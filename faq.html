Frequently Asked Questions

1) What is MUN ?

MUN(Model United Nations) is an academic simulation of United Nations in it’s form and content. MUN is an initiative to engage youth in healthy discourse and expose them to current events, topics in international relations, diplomacy and the United Nations agenda.

2) Who can register for JMUN 2015?

Owing to the fact that Jaypee Model United Nations is an academic simulation, students from any background can participate i.e. from high school, graduation or post-graduation level.

3) What if I have never participated in MUN? What if I don’t know the rules?

All the required training will be provided. Moreover a delegate handbook and background guides will be provided during the course which will further give you an insight into the committee and agenda.

4) How can I be a part of JMUN 2015?

Participants can get themselves registered as a delegate of a particular country or a particular leader. Link to application

5) What are the different committees simulated at JMUN 2015?

JMUN 2015 is having a total five committees this time, which are as follows :

  • General Assembly-DISEC
  • International Atomic Energy Agency
  • Human Rights Council
  • UNCSTD
  • Security Council

6) What is the registration fee for JMUN?

Registration fee for delegates is 900 INR and journalists is 700 INR respectively, which includes delegate kit, snacks, lunch, certificates, prize money and trophies.

7) How can I remain updated with the activities of JMUN 2015?

You may reach us on web at: https://www.jmun.co.in. You may also follow us on facebook at https://www.facebook.com/JaypeeMUN

8) How can I reach the venue?

JMUN 2015 will take place in JIIT, A-10, Sec-62, NOIDA. Moreover further details regarding the venue can be found here.

9) How can I pay the registration fee?

You can pay the fee via net transfer or via our online payment portal which shall be activated at a later stage. All those wishing to pay offline they may make reservations for the same once the allotments are released. Our cash collection centres will be set up across Delhi NCR region. Details regarding the payment will be released during the course of time.

10) How can I contact the Organizing Committee?

You can drop your queries to jpmun2015@gmail.com

11) What transportation facilities will be provided to the participants?

Bus facility from Kaushambi Metro Sation, Noida City Centre Metro Station and our respective accomodation to the venue of conference will be provided to the participants on the conference days, timings to be declared with the itinerary of the conference on our official facebook page and website as well.
